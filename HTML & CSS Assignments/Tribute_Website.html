The Indian Army Para SF, also known as Para Commandos, is a special forces unit of the Indian Army. They are highly trained and specialized in conducting special operations such as direct action, hostage rescue, and counter-terrorism.

The Para SF was formed in 1966 in response to the growing need for a specialized force that could operate behind enemy lines. Since then, they have been involved in numerous operations, both domestic and international.
